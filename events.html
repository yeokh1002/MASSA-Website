Our Events

We aim to promote Malaysian and Singaporean culture, foster meaningful connections, and provide academic, professional, and personal support to our members.

Cultural Night

An immersive celebration of diverse cultures through performances, cuisines, and exhibits.

Study Abroad Panel

An informative session featuring firsthand experiences and practical advice from students who have embarked on study abroad journeys.

Food Festival

A culinary extravaganza showcasing a tantalizing array of flavors, aromas, and cuisines from Malaysia and Singapore.

Charity Run

A heart-pumping event that combines fitness with philanthropy, bringing together participants of all ages to run.

A patriotic and joyous event commemorating the country's freedom and unity.

Freshman Welcoming Night

An exciting event designed to introduce new students to campus life, foster connections with peers.
